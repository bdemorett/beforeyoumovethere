About Before You Move There

Before You Move There helps people research places before making a real relocation decision.

What this site is for

The site focuses on practical relocation questions: cost, housing, neighborhoods, jobs, commute, weather, schools, parks, safety concerns, and the tradeoffs that affect daily life after the move.

The goal is not to make every place sound appealing. The goal is to help you understand whether a place fits your household, budget, work, climate tolerance, and routine.

How to use it

Start broad with a state guide, narrow into a city guide, then use data pages and future comparison pages to check whether the numbers support the lifestyle story.

Every moving decision still needs current local research, in-person visits where possible, and professional advice for legal, tax, real estate, or financial questions.
