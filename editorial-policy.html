Editorial Policy

Our guides are written to help readers make relocation decisions with clearer context and fewer surprises.

How guides are created

Guides combine public data, official sources, local geography, market context, and editorial analysis focused on moving decisions. We aim to explain tradeoffs, not rank places with one-size-fits-all claims.

Data pages should cite source types and be updated when important numbers become stale or when a reader flags a useful correction.

Corrections and independence

If we find an error, we update the page. Partner links or advertising should not determine editorial conclusions. Sponsored or affiliate material should be clearly labeled and separated from guide analysis.

This site does not provide legal, tax, real estate, financial, or safety advice. Readers should verify current details before making decisions.
